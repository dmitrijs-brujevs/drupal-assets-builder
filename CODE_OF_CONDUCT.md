# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Use welcoming and inclusive language, respect differing viewpoints, accept constructive feedback,
focus on what is best for the community, and show empathy toward other community members.
Harassment, trolling, personal attacks, and publishing private information without permission are
unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately through the repository owner's GitHub contact channels.
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently
ban contributors for behavior they consider inappropriate.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
